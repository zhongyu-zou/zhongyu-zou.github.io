I am Zhongyu Zou, a biomedical researcher currently based in Basel, Switzerland. I hold a B.S. in chemical biology from Peking University. After that, I did my Ph.D. in chemistry with Prof. Chuan He in the University of Chicago from 2018 to 2024. I worked with Prof. Hans Clevers and Prof. Randall Platt in Roche and ETH Zurich as a postdoc fellow from 2025. 

I try to develop novel methods to understand the functions of small molecules in the human body, especially the GI tract and liver, using organoid models. I am especially interested in revealing new mechanisms RNAs utilize to regulate cellular activities.
